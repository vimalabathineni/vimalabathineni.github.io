Vimala Bathineni

I am a passionate horticulturist with expertise in crop improvement, sustainable production, and carbon sequestration. My work is rooted in advancing plant propagation, flower seed breeding, and seed science to enhance productivity and environmental resilience. With hands-on experience in high-density planting, pest management, and cover cropping, I develop innovative solutions that balance agricultural productivity with ecological sustainability.

After relocating to Denmark, I expanded my expertise by learning artificial intelligence, deep learning, and convolutional neural networks. I am excited to integrate this knowledge with my horticultural background to create innovative approaches that support and benefit farmers.
