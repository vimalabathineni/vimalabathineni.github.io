Vinay Gogineni

I am an Assistant Professor at the Applied Artificial Intelligence and Data Science Unit, The Mærsk Mc-Kinney Møller Institute, University of Southern Denmark (since July 2023).

My research focuses on Artificial Intelligence (AI), particularly in Deep Learning, Federated Learning (also known as Decentralized Machine Learning), Graph Machine Learning, and Machine Unlearning. I apply these technologies in Healthcare, the Industrial Internet-of-Things (IIoT), and Fusion Energy. My work aims to develop novel AI algorithms that enhance performance, protect privacy, and promote fairness by mitigating bias.

Aside from the fundamental research in AI, I have also been developing AI-driven early detection systems for Colorectal Cancer, Cardio Calcification, Cervical Cancer, and Dementia, striving to improve patient outcomes through advanced diagnostic tools.
