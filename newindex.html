Andres Barinas-Forero

Hello! I am a Colombian economist with a deep interest in applied microeconomics, particularly in behavioral economics, political economy, public economics, and experimental economics.

I hold both an M.A. and a B.A. in Economics from Universidad de los Andes in Bogotá, Colombia.

Currently, I am working as a Pre-Doctoral Research Fellow at the Research Department of the Inter-American Development Bank. Previously, I worked as a Research Consultant at the World Bank, and as a Research Assistant at Universidad de los Andes and Universidad del Rosario in Bogotá, Colombia.
