Our Project Learn to Unlearn: Towards Responsible AI (TRAI) received funding from Novo Nordisk Fonden.

Vimala Bathineni

From September 2016 to August 2023, I worked as an Assistant Professor/Scientist at the Cashew research station in Bapatla (AICRP on Cashew) and Horticulture Research Station in Anantapuramu (AICRP on Arid Zone Fruits), under Dr. Y.S.R. Horticultural University.

My work focused on research in production management, pest and disease control, training and pruning techniques, developing and identifying new varieties, commercializing released varieties, and nursery management—bridging the gap between scientific research and practical solutions for farmers.

After relocating to Denmark, I expanded my expertise by learning artificial intelligence, deep learning, and convolutional neural networks. I am excited to integrate this knowledge with my horticultural background to create innovative approaches that support and benefit farmers.

I’ve shared my research results through a variety of platforms, including research articles, book chapters, newspapers, bulletins, training programs, Kisan Melas, TV and radio, as well as YouTube videos. One of my YouTube videos even received around 440K views, showing how far and wide my work has reached.
